CN Tower Challenge

Story

The Flappy Bird game is a simple yet addictive experience that has captivated millions of players around the world. Flappy Bird is a 2D side-scrolling mobile game developed by Vietnamese game developer Dong Nguyen and released in 2013. Flappy Bird is a mobile/web game in which a player controls a bird by tapping the screen/spacebar to make it fly through a series of obstacles, avoiding collisions to achieve the highest score possible. The initiative aligns perfectly with our course, as it integrates various aspects of web development, game design, and user experience, while providing a practical application of the skills acquired.

About our version

In the heart of Toronto, a brave bird named Skyler leads a flock on a daring adventure, discovering a hidden world above the clouds surrounding the CN Tower. Legend speaks of a mystical realm with untold treasures, reachable only by navigating challenging skies. As players guide Skyler through floating platforms and airborne obstacles inspired by the iconic tower's architecture, they collect shimmering feathers unlocking special abilities. The journey culminates in breaching the mystical realm, revealing a breathtaking world above the clouds, where the birds celebrate their triumphant ascent. CN Tower Soar combines thrilling gameplay with stunning visuals, offering players a chance to uncover the secrets of the avian legacy guarded by the towering icon.
